01 — About

From spray cans to full interiors.

Blesson started with letters on concrete and has carried that same energy into murals, portraits, and full commercial interiors. Every wall gets treated like a canvas — the scale just changes.

The work ranges from freehand graffiti pieces and character murals to fine-art portraits, cafe interior schemes, and branded campaign walls for clients who want their space to say something.
